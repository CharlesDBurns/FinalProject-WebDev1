About Us

To establish a level of trust with users and fellow campers alike, we Knights of The Round Tent like to tell you a little bit about ourselves and the organization as a whole. As stated on the homepage, our mission is to bring easy access to camping resources and ancient European history and culture with engaging events and fun facts. Our knights are here to share their knowledge with fellow appreciators of the Medieval Ages. But that's not all they do, they are also here to help with all of your camping needs, whether it's finding a park to camp out on or tips to help make your camping trip the best it can be.


We're here to help your camping experience be pleasurable and just the way you want it to be. Constantly updating, the knights here at Knights of The Round Tent make sure that you always have the latest information on Medieval-themed events, parks around the country and camping rules and tips to keep your experience pleasing. We constantly seek out Medieval events to show to our users as well as non-medieval events for those who rather attend those instead. This site is one of the best sources of camping information, Medieval events and lists of parks, where knights work tirelessly to give you all the information you need for your camping adventure to start out with a stunning victory!

Beginnings

It all started when Charles was to organize a camping trip for him and his friends to go on, he was responsible for planning the trip. They wanted a good place with a great view to camp out at and medieval events either on the way or in the area to attend as they were extremely interested in knights and castles. Upon looking for camping spots and medieveal events, Charles found it hard to orchestrate, it was then he asked, “why can't there be a site for both camping and medieval events? Why can't there be place where fellow medieval enthusiasts who love the outdoors can find the information their looking for all in one place?” Thus the journey of the Knights of The Round Tent began. Charles set out to create just such a website, learning more about the Medieval Period as he gathered historians and his freinds' knowledge on camping, and that's when he founded Knights of The Round Tent.
