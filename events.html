Events

Jousting- 9/6/2022

Description:

Do you have what it takes to beat the finest knights of the land? In the Renaissance Festival, jousting is an available activity in which two knights would ride at each other on horseback and try to knock the other off. Although, this event used to be to the death back in Medieval Times with sharper lances, it was later changed to not be so. In this reenactment, volunteers will be given a tutorial in the basics, proper form, help with putting on armor and riding horseback with lance and shield. This event takes place in South Carolina.

Autumn 4k Charity Run- 9/25/2022

Description:

A 4k run to raise money for charities for clothes and food for the homeless, food for the starving in Africa and for cancer research. Sponsered by numerous companies and organizations including Knights of The Round Tent, it is a 4k run or roughly 2.49 miles with the goal of raising money for these noble causes. Best for beginners to train before the day of the run, if they plan on paritcipating, unless they just want to run with their friends and family. This pleasant autumn activity lets people see the gorgeous autumn scenery in the countryside of Georgia.

Spookfest!- 10/31/2022

Description:

Come to Idaho, if you dare! Where Spookfest turns it into the scariest state in October. Spookfest turns Idaho into the embodiment of Halloween as it spares no expense in giving every street the Halloween spirit. The main center of attration being the Spookfest HQ, where an entire festival is set up for one day only, with minigames, rides and events that will leave you shaking in your boots. It also features a great jack o' lantern carving contest with a $500 cash prize. A yearly event that is sure to put the Halloween spirit into you, that you'll be sure not to miss!

Sparring- 11/5/2022

Description:

Are you a real knight? Test your skills against Medieval recreatiionists who train like knights back in the age itself. Protected with suits of armor of course, you'll clash swords like the knights of old in an authentic reenactment with the exception of safety. Volunteers are given a crash course in the basics of combat back in the Medieval Ages along with help putting on armor and basic stances with correct forms. This is an experience that few would regret and many would remember for a long time to come. This event takes place in Colorado.
