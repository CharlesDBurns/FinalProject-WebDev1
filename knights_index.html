Knights of The Round Tent

Never fear, the Knights of Camping are here!

Here for all your camping needs, the Knights of the Round Tent is an organization founded by Charles D. Burns, consisting of avid historians and camping enthusiasts who love to share their knowledge of both the outdoors and the Medieval Ages. Each knight is an expert in the history of all medieval things with extensive knowledge on camping techniques and practices that are sure to help in any camping experience. The mission of this organization is to bring easy access to camping resources and ancient European history and culture with engaging events and fun facts.
Knights of the Round Tent is the perfect place to find prime camping locations as well as Medieval-themed events from across the nation, from jousting to reenactments of tales of old. This is also the place to learn just what you need to start off your camping experience off right while also learning fun facts about the ways in which people did things back in the Medieval Ages. All of this and more, on the Medieval-themed camping site known as Knights of the Round Tent.
